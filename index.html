About LP

Lignum Pulse is a web design and branding company intended to provide Web Development and Maintenance for a variety of organizations, entrepreneurs, charities and schools. Our goal is to help organiztions geared toward increasing social good gain a strong web presence.

By patronizing LP, you help alleviate the cost of a website for a local charity or other social organization to gain web exposure.

Led by a team of young executives, LP strives to act as a form of consolidation for the marketplace; refining the standard of web-design and positively contributing to the community from which it was born.
